Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the FSK Comm mobile application provided by BeepSystems.

FSK Comm is designed to operate without requiring account registration and without collecting personal or sensitive user data for BeepSystems.

Information We Access, Collect, Use, and Share

BeepSystems does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal or sensitive user data from FSK Comm.

  • No user account is required to use the app.
  • No personal information is submitted to BeepSystems through the app.
  • No personal or sensitive user data is shared with third parties by BeepSystems.
  • No advertising, profiling, or data brokerage is performed by BeepSystems through the app.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because BeepSystems does not collect personal or sensitive user data through FSK Comm, there is no server-side personal data retained by BeepSystems and no account deletion workflow is required.

Security

Because BeepSystems does not receive or store personal or sensitive user data from the app, there is no such data held on BeepSystems systems for this app.

Children's Privacy

FSK Comm is not directed to children under 13. BeepSystems does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

Changes to This Policy

BeepSystems may update this Privacy Policy if the app's data practices change. Any update will be published on this page before or when the change takes effect.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact BeepSystems at contact@beep.systems.
